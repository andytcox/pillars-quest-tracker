The Hollowing of the Dyrwood[edit | edit source]

The Hollowing of the Dyrwood is the main quest line in the game.

Act 1[edit | edit source]

  • A Moment's Respite
  • The Ruins of Cilant Ls
  • The Gilded Vale
  • Visions and Whispers
  • The Old Watcher

Act 2[edit | edit source]

  • Never Far from the Queen
  • The Man Who Waits
  • Undying Heritage
  • Through Death's Gate
  • The Hermit of Hadret House

Act 3[edit | edit source]

  • The Assassin at Large
  • Council of Stars
  • Court of the Penitents

Act 4[edit | edit source]

  • Memories of the Ancients

End[edit | edit source]

See Endings.

The White March[edit | edit source]

The White March is an additional main quest line added by the expansion The White March - Part I. It is integrated into the main campaign, so you don't have to start a separate game.

Part 1[edit | edit source]

  • Durgan's Battery
  • The Ogre Matron
  • The Recluse of the White March
  • The White Forge

Bounties[edit | edit source]

Bounty hunter missions are a special type of task, meant for players who are looking for some challenging combat. They are given in batches, and you only get the next batch once you have completed all of the current batch from the same commissioner.
